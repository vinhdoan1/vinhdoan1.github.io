Geom

Geom is a reimagined version of the classic game of Snake. This retro, black and white styles game's basic premise is to keep your snake tail long enough under a certain amount of time. There is also Challenge Mode, which tests the player's snake ability through several different challenges with goals.

2048 Flap

Based on the two hit games, Flappy Birds and 2048, 2048 Flap combines both into one suspenseful, thinking game. The board is split into 2 sides, one for tapping and jumping your block, and the other for swiping to move the column of blocks up or down. Aim to the 2048 block, and go for the high score!
